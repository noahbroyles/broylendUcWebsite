Why I chose this Site theme

        I decided to use a two column layout for this site so that my nav could be fixed on the side and not at the bottom or top of the main content. I use two different fonts for headers and main content, and a few main colors, black, white, red, and green for the link to the current page. I like the smooth dark look of the site overall with the links standing out in a dark red. I like the dark theme because it is easy on your eyes when you are reading.
