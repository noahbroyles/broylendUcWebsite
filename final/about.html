About:

        In my final project, I continued with my midterm topic, which was composed of details about three different operating systems, MacOS, Ubuntu, and Windows 10. I compare various parts of the three operating systems and give background information about each of them.

Color Theme:

        I used a dark color scheme because I am a fan of the overall look of light text on a dark background, and because it is easy to focus on. I used red and green as highlight colors for links and hovering effects. My headers have pure white colored font, and my main paragraph text is white with a slight mix-in of red.

Layout:

        My site is fully responsive with @media queries. It has a three-column layout for larger screens, with a navigation area on the left side, Reddit Links on the right, and the main content in the center. When the viewportâ€™s width is decreased, the site scales back to a single column with a nav bar at the top and the Reddit links at the bottom.
